About Us

TATVA 2022

TATVA is the annual techno-cultural festival of BNMIT organized by its students. TATVA attracts the best technical talent to compete with each other and prove their competence. TATVA brings creativity and freshness in its events by introducing at least one new event every year to bring in new challenges among the participants, the fest culminates with a Mega Event. We have a lot of events lined up, about which we will brief you shortly. Sit back and enjoy the show, we have a lot to share.

Key highlights

Hogathon

A mega food eating competition in which participants will be given food items which they will have to gobble within a certain time limit to beat their opponent.

Minute to win it!

A contest in which participants take part in various 60-second challenges. These time constricted tasks involve common everyday tasks using household objects that test the strategic skill of the final winner.

Photography

A Theme specific event in which participants using blend of technical skills and an artistic eye can click vivid pictures by using either phones or cameras. The team that possesses pictures that depict the given theme in the most expressive way possible win the challenge.

Reel It

This contest is a face-off between the most creative squads of the college. Each team will be given a specific time within which they have to design and produce 30-second reels related to the given theme in the most innovative way possible to win the contest.

Treasure Hunt

An event that consists of various puzzles and riddles to be solved to obtain the final treasure. Participants will be given clues at several stages around the campus, which lead you on to the next clue testing your knowledge about the BNMIT campus.

Quiz

The quiz will comprise 3 rounds in which the participants will be asked a set of questions related to various genres. Each round will also be an eliminator and only the best of minds shall pass. The duo to get through all the rounds and solve the final mystery will stand as the reigning champion.

VALORANT

A 5v5 character–based tactical FPS where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. The event comprises challenges of extreme of hand to eye co-ordination and tests every player’s communication and teamwork skills.

F1

F1 2021 is the official video game of the 2021 Formula One and Formula 2 championships developed by Code masters and published by EA Sports. Each player will be allowed to race as their favorite racer with a realistic racing simulator. The video game is based precisely on how the Formula 1 races are held and each player will have to be precise with their decision making on and off the track.

Mock IPL Auction

It is a real-time mock IPL Auction every team gets a fixed amount of virtual money to bid on international T20 Players. After a thorough analysis, they will need to strategize which players they need to bid more money on and create their fantasy cricket teams.

Robo Race

This a two round racing event filled with exciting race tracks along with a specially designed workshop! Participants will receive a hands-on learning experience on designing your very own chassis for a robo car and getting it laser cut along with the basics of coding on a micro controller! After which they will Assemble thier robo car, and finally taking it to the race tracks!
